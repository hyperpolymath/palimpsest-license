Technical Excellence

Synthetic Lineage Tags

Machine-readable metadata that traces AI outputs back to source material

  • JSON-LD, XML, and NDJSON formats
  • Embedded in AI model outputs
  • Quantum-resistant cryptographic signatures
  • Interoperable with Dublin Core and schema.org

AI Boundary Declaration Protocol (AIBDP)

Technical framework for declaring AI usage boundaries

  • `.well-known/aibdp.json` manifest
  • `GenAI-Consent` HTTP headers
  • DNS TXT records for domain-wide policies
  • HTML metadata tags for individual resources

Legal credibility points:

  • Why Dutch Law: Netherlands has strong tradition of balancing creator rights with innovation. Dutch copyright law is well-developed for digital works. EU compliance ensures broad applicability.
  • Why Scottish Courts: Scotland's Court of Session has expertise in complex digital rights cases. The dual-jurisdiction model provides legal certainty whilst maintaining cultural sensitivity (Scotland's own experience with cultural preservation informs its judicial approach).
  • Governance: Unlike proprietary licenses, Palimpsest is community-governed. No single corporation controls it. The Stewardship Council includes creators, legal experts, technologists, and cultural advocates.
  • Tested Framework: v0.3 has been in use since 2024. v0.4 incorporates lessons learned and expands AI protections.
