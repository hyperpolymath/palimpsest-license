== Layer Descriptions

=== License Core
The canonical license text and its normative exhibits.

=== Specification Layer
Formal definitions, provenance rules, ethical constraints, and SCM manifest specifications.

=== Frameworks Layer
Jurisdictional analyses grounding the license in real legal systems.

=== Strategy Layer
Legal strategy, honest broker profiles, policy one‑pagers, and test‑case frameworks.

=== Outreach Layer
Operational engagement with law firms, academics, and digital rights organisations.

=== Historical Layer
Preservation of lineage and interpretive history.

== Governance

The Palimpsest Stewardship Council oversees:

* normative changes  
* jurisdictional alignment  
* legal strategy  
* outreach priorities
