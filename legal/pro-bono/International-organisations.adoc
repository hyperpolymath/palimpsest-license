= International Pro Bono Legal Organisations
:toc: macro

These organisations provide global expertise relevant to licensing, digital rights, and cultural protection.

toc::[]

== Creative Commons

* **Type:** Global licensing organisation  
* **Expertise:** Open licensing, copyright reform  
* **Relevance:** Compatibility assessment and collaboration  

== Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)

* **Type:** Digital rights NGO  
* **Expertise:** Platform accountability, AI, copyright  
* **Relevance:** Policy advocacy and legal strategy  

== UNESCO – Cultural Rights Division

* **Type:** UN agency  
* **Expertise:** Cultural rights, heritage protection  
* **Relevance:** Alignment with Palimpsest’s cultural and narrative focus  

== PIIPA – Public Interest Intellectual Property Advisors

* **Type:** Global pro bono network  
* **Expertise:** IP support for public interest projects  
* **Relevance:** Potential pro bono matchmaker  

== WIPO – Copyright and Creative Industries Sector

* **Type:** International organisation  
* **Expertise:** Copyright harmonisation  
* **Relevance:** Long‑term policy alignment
