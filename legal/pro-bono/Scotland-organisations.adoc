= Scotland Pro Bono Legal Organisations
:toc: macro

Scotland operates under a distinct legal system.  
These organisations provide expertise relevant to Palimpsest’s jurisdictional analysis.

toc::[]

== University of Edinburgh – SCRIPT Centre

* **Type:** Academic research centre  
* **Expertise:** Technology law, AI ethics, digital rights  
* **Relevance:** Academic validation and policy alignment  

== University of Glasgow – CREATe

* **Type:** Research centre  
* **Expertise:** Copyright, cultural rights, creative industries  
* **Relevance:** Strong alignment with Palimpsest’s narrative and cultural focus  

== Scottish Legal Aid Board – Pro Bono Unit

* **Type:** Public body  
* **Expertise:** Legal coordination  
* **Relevance:** Potential gateway to Scottish IP practitioners  

== Shepherd and Wedderburn (Scotland)

* **Type:** Law firm  
* **Expertise:** IP, technology, media  
* **Relevance:** Potential pro bono partner
