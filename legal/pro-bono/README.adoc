= Pro Bono Legal Outreach – Overview
:toc: macro
:toclevels: 3

The `legal/pro-bono/` directory contains all materials required to coordinate pro bono legal outreach for the Palimpsest‑MPL License.  
It includes organisation profiles, contact databases, outreach trackers, and engagement letter templates.

This subsystem supports legal validation, academic engagement, and policy advocacy across multiple jurisdictions.

toc::[]

== Directory Structure

----
pro-bono/
├── README.adoc
├── organisations/
├── contacts/
└── letters/
----

== Organisations

`organisations/` contains structured profiles of legal organisations across key jurisdictions:

* Netherlands (primary jurisdiction)
* United Kingdom (England & Wales)
* Scotland
* International (Creative Commons, EFF, UNESCO, etc.)

Each profile includes:

* mission and focus areas  
* relevant expertise  
* suitability for Palimpsest outreach  
* contact pathways  

== Contacts

`contacts/` contains:

* `master-database.adoc` — comprehensive list of 60+ organisations  
* `priority-contacts.adoc` — Tier 1 outreach targets  
* `outreach-tracker.adoc` — per‑organisation tracking template  

These files support coordinated outreach and follow‑up.

== Letters

`letters/` contains engagement letter templates for:

* academic institutions  
* law firms  
* digital rights organisations  
* Creative Commons  
* general introductions  
* IViR Amsterdam (priority outreach)  

Each template is designed for customisation and includes:

* purpose of engagement  
* materials provided  
* next steps  
* Palimpsest context  

== Outreach Workflow

. Identify target organisation  
. Review organisation profile  
. Select appropriate letter template  
. Customise template  
. Send outreach email  
. Log outreach in `master-database.adoc`  
. Track follow‑ups in `outreach-tracker.adoc`  

== Confidentiality

* Organisation profiles → public  
* Letter templates → public  
* Master database → internal  
* Outreach status → internal  
* Legal opinions → confidential (Council only)
