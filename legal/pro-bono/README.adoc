= Pro Bono Legal Outreach – Overview
:toc: macro
:toclevels: 2

This directory contains all materials related to pro bono legal outreach for the Palimpsest‑MPL License project.  
It includes organisation profiles, contact databases, engagement letter templates, and outreach tracking tools.

toc::[]

== Structure

----
pro-bono/
├── README.adoc
├── organisations/
├── contacts/
└── letters/
----

=== organisations/
Profiles of legal organisations across jurisdictions:

* UK
* Scotland
* Netherlands (primary jurisdiction)
* International (Creative Commons, EFF, UNESCO, etc.)

=== contacts/
* `master-database.adoc` — full contact database  
* `priority-contacts.adoc` — Tier 1 outreach targets  
* `outreach-tracker.adoc` — per‑organisation tracking template  

=== letters/
Engagement letter templates for:

* academic partnerships  
* law firms  
* digital rights organisations  
* Creative Commons  
* general introductions  
* IViR Amsterdam (priority outreach)

== Workflow

. Identify target organisation  
. Review organisation profile  
. Select appropriate letter template  
. Customise template  
. Send outreach email  
. Track status in `contacts/master-database.adoc`  
. Update follow‑ups in `outreach-tracker.adoc`  

== Confidentiality

* Organisation profiles → public  
* Letter templates → public  
* Master database → internal  
* Outreach status → internal  
* Legal opinions → confidential (Council only)
