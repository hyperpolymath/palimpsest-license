# Pro Bono Legal Organisations - International

## Overview
International organisations provide crucial support for the Palimpsest License's global reach, cross-border enforcement, and harmonisation across different legal systems. This document focuses on organisations with expertise in international IP law, digital rights, AI governance, and creative commons.

## International Legal Considerations

### Key Questions for International Experts
1. **Conflict of laws:** How does Dutch law license interact with US fair use, EU DSM Directive, etc.?
2. **Enforcement:** Can Palimpsest license be enforced across borders?
3. **AI training:** How do different jurisdictions treat TDM exceptions (US fair use vs EU opt-out)?
4. **Moral rights:** Jurisdictional variations (strong in EU, weak in US)
5. **Platform liability:** How do platforms in different jurisdictions handle custom licenses?

### Harmonisation Opportunities
- Align with Creative Commons approach (jurisdiction-specific versions)
- Learn from GPL's international enforcement (Software Freedom Law Center)
- Consider model contract clauses for cross-border licensing
- Engage WIPO for international IP harmonisation

The international landscape offers rich opportunities for the Palimpsest License to gain legitimacy, expertise, and global reach. Priority should be:
