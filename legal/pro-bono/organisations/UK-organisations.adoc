= UK Pro Bono Legal Organisations
:toc: macro

This document profiles UK‑based organisations relevant to Palimpsest‑MPL legal outreach.  
The UK is a secondary jurisdiction with distinct copyright and moral rights frameworks.

toc::[]

== Open Rights Group (ORG)

* **Type:** Digital rights NGO  
* **Expertise:** Platform accountability, privacy, copyright reform  
* **Relevance:** Strong alignment with Palimpsest’s ethical‑use and provenance goals  
* **Engagement Path:** Policy team, legal research unit  

== LawWorks (UK Pro Bono)

* **Type:** National pro bono network  
* **Expertise:** Connecting projects with law firms  
* **Relevance:** Gateway to IP/tech law firms  
* **Engagement Path:** Project intake form  

== University of Oxford – IP Research Centre

* **Type:** Academic centre  
* **Expertise:** Copyright, moral rights, AI governance  
* **Relevance:** Academic validation and expert commentary  
* **Engagement Path:** Faculty outreach  

== University of Cambridge – Centre for IP and Information Law (CIPIL)

* **Type:** Academic centre  
* **Expertise:** Copyright, TDM, digital rights  
* **Relevance:** High‑credibility academic review  
* **Engagement Path:** Research leads  

== DLA Piper (UK)

* **Type:** Law firm  
* **Expertise:** Technology, IP, AI regulation  
* **Relevance:** Pro bono potential for license review  
* **Engagement Path:** Pro bono coordinator
