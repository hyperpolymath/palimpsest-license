= Dutch Translation Brief – Palimpsest‑MPL License
:toc: macro

This brief provides guidance for certified legal translators preparing the official Dutch translation of the Palimpsest‑MPL License.  
Dutch law governs the license; therefore, the Dutch version must be precise, authoritative, and legally enforceable.

toc::[]

== Objectives

* Produce a legally accurate Dutch translation of:
  - PALIMPSEST-MPL-1.0.txt
  - All normative exhibits
  - Required definitions and terminology
* Maintain fidelity to:
  - Dutch copyright law (Auteurswet)
  - Dutch civil law terminology
  - MPL‑2.0 structure and intent
* Ensure compatibility with:
  - EU DSM Directive
  - Dutch TDM exceptions
  - Dutch moral rights doctrine

== Translation Requirements

=== 1. Legal Accuracy
Use established Dutch legal terminology for:
* auteursrecht  
* persoonlijkheidsrechten  
* verveelvoudiging  
* openbaarmaking  
* licentievoorwaarden  
* beperkingen en uitzonderingen  

=== 2. Structural Fidelity
* Preserve section numbering and hierarchy.
* Preserve exhibit references.
* Preserve defined terms exactly.

=== 3. Terminology Consistency
Use the Dutch–English legal lexicon in:
`legal/translations/dutch-english-legal-lexicon.md`

=== 4. Translator Notes
Include translator footnotes where:
* Dutch law diverges from US/EU terminology  
* MPL‑2.0 concepts require clarification  
* Cultural or narrative terms require nuance  

== Deliverables

* Dutch translation (canonical `.txt`)
* Annotated version with translator notes
* Summary of interpretive decisions
* Certification of translation accuracy
