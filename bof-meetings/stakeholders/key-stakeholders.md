## Engagement Strategy

### Prioritisation

**Tier 1 (Critical)**:
- Direct invitations
- One-on-one briefings
- Involvement in organizing team

**Tier 2 (Important)**:
- Targeted email invitations
- Inclusion in pre-BoF webinars
- Follow-up after BoF session

**Tier 3 (Broad)**:
- Mailing list announcements
- Social media outreach
- General conference networking

### Outreach Timeline

**8 weeks before**: Tier 1 personal invitations
**6 weeks before**: Tier 2 targeted outreach
**4 weeks before**: Tier 3 broad announcements
**2 weeks before**: Reminder emails to confirmed attendees
**1 week after**: Follow-up with all attendees
**Ongoing**: Relationship building with key stakeholders

### Tracking

Use stakeholder database (spreadsheet or CRM) to track:
- Contact information
- Conference(s) of interest
- Engagement level (attended, expressed interest, committed to action)
- Follow-up actions and status
- Notes on interests and concerns

## Notes

**Diversity and Inclusion**: Ensure outreach prioritizes:
- Geographic diversity (Global South representation)
- Gender diversity and LGBTQIA+ inclusion
- Racial and ethnic diversity
- Marginalized creator communities
- Variety of organizational types (not just large NGOs)

**Avoiding Conflicts of Interest**: Be transparent about:
- Industry representatives with vested interests
- Potential opposition from AI companies
- Platform dependencies and relationships

**GDPR Compliance**: Obtain consent for:
- Adding to mailing lists
- Storing contact information
- Sharing information with partners
